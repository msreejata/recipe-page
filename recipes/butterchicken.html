Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

500g boneless chicken (cut into pieces) | 2 tbsp butter | 1 cup tomato puree | ½ cup heavy cream | ¼ cup yogurt | 1 onion (finely chopped) | 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste | 1 tsp garam masala | 1 tsp cumin powder | 1 tsp coriander powder | 1 tsp red chili powder | ½ tsp turmeric powder | 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) | Salt to taste | Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, half of the ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it marinate for 30 minutes (if possible).
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until browned. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pan, add butter and sauté onions until golden. Add the remaining ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Simmer the Sauce: Add tomato puree, cumin, coriander, garam masala, chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Combine & Finish: Add the cooked chicken, heavy cream, and kasuri methi. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish & Serve: Sprinkle fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
