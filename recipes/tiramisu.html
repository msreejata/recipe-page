Tiramisu

Ingredients:

250g mascarpone cheese | 3 egg yolks | 3 tbsp sugar | 1 cup heavy cream | 1 cup brewed espresso (cooled) | 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional) | 200g ladyfinger biscuits | 2 tbsp cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Cream: Whisk egg yolks and sugar until creamy. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth.
  2. Whip the Cream: In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until soft peaks form, then fold it into the mascarpone mixture.
  3. Soak the Ladyfingers: Mix espresso and coffee liqueur. Dip ladyfingers quickly into the coffee (do not soak).
  4. Layer the Tiramisu: Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in a dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over them. Repeat with another layer.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  6. Serve: Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
