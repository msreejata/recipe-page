Mac n Cheese

Home Ingredients:
    • 250g mascarpone cheese 3 egg yolks 3 tbsp sugar 1 cup heavy cream 1 cup brewed espresso (cooled) 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional) 200g ladyfinger biscuits 2 tbsp cocoa powder
    • Instructions:
    • Prepare the Cream: Whisk egg yolks and sugar until creamy. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth. Whip the Cream: In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until soft peaks form, then fold it into the mascarpone mixture. Soak the Ladyfingers: Mix espresso and coffee liqueur. Dip ladyfingers quickly into the coffee (do not soak). Layer the Tiramisu: Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in a dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over them. Repeat with another layer. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Serve: Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
