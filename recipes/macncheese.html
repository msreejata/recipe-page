Mac n Cheese

Home Ingredients:
    • 2 cups elbow macaroni 2 tbsp butter 2 tbsp all-purpose flour 2 cups milk (warm) 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese ½ tsp mustard powder (optional) Salt & black pepper to taste ½ tsp paprika (optional)
    • Instructions:
    • Cook the Macaroni: Boil macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. Make the Cheese Sauce: Melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Add flour and whisk for 1-2 minutes. Gradually add warm milk while stirring to avoid lumps. Melt the Cheese: Add mustard powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir in cheddar cheese until melted and smooth. Combine: Mix the cooked macaroni into the sauce. Stir well and serve warm.
