Mac n Cheese

Ingredients:

2 cups elbow macaroni | 2 tbsp butter | 2 tbsp all-purpose flour | 2 cups milk (warm) | 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese | ½ tsp mustard powder (optional) | Salt & black pepper to taste | ½ tsp paprika (optional)

Instructions:

  1. the Macaroni: Boil macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Cheese Sauce: Melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Add flour and whisk for 1-2 minutes. Gradually add warm milk while stirring to avoid lumps.
  3. Melt the Cheese: Add mustard powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir in cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  4. Combine: Mix the cooked macaroni into the sauce. Stir well and serve warm.
